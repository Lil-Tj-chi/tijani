In 2002, during District Pastor Emmanuel's one-year absence,

Robert Agyapomaa assumed pastoral duties and oversaw the establishment of four local churches:

Pigfarm, Sahara, Mamobi, and Agbogbloshie, with the support of ministers of faith including

Elder Gilbert Ameku, Elder Essuman, Deaconess Esther Omari, Deaconess Esther Sarfo, and Pastor Adjetey,

which marked the beginning of the church's growth.

The mission of the church is to serve as a beacon of hope,

love and spiritual guidance

for members and the broader community
